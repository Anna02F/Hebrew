Who we are

Dream Seekers Academy is a premier advisory focused on providing comprehensive admissions support for students seeking entry into the world’s elite undergraduate and graduate universities.

Our team includes London Business School, Harvard and MIT alumni with admissions experience. Each of our admissions specialists has a degree from a world-class institution at either the undergraduate or graduate level.

A Different Approach

We believe that the approach of forcibly molding students into “Ivy League prospects” is misguided. Instead, empowering students to pursue their passions by developing their interpersonal, communication and leadership skills naturally turns them into elite University prospects.

We tailor the experience to the student by specifically addressing their weaknesses and developing their strengths with one-on-one guidance. Through the process – students cultivate the skills that help them become more confident, competent and self-reliant individuals.

Results

Over the past three years, we've worked with clients across North America, South America and Asia.

100% of our clients are admitted to at least one of their top choice schools.

A few of our admission results in the last year include Stanford, University of Chicago, and Johns Hopkins.
