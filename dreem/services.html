Services

We work with students from around the work who are seeking admissions support for their applications to the world’s elite undergraduate and graduate universities in the US/UK.

Your application is the showcase for your brand. Everything from your Facebook profile to your personal statement communicates your brand to schools. The better the communication, the better your chances of admission. We offer customized packages with this principle in mind.

Process

  • Step 01
    Meet & Greet

    In our first meeting, we will get to know more about you and the elements that make up your personal narrative. Based on an assessment of your abilities, we will craft an overarching strategy to work on your weaknesses and develop your strengths.

    We emphasize core skills development, particularly – interpersonal, communication and leadership. Later, we craft an application strategy that encompasses school selection, essay construction, recommendations and outreach.

  • Step 02
    App Design

    In this stage, we help tailor the execution of your application strategy to each of your target schools. We help you select the experiences that best fit your application essays.

    Based on our discussions, you write the essays and submit them for review. We exchange drafts and revisions of individual essays until both of us are happy with the product.

  • Step 03
    Integration

    This is where it all comes together. We do a final review of your application as a whole. After any last tweaking, your application is finalized.

  • Step 04
    Interview

    Practice makes perfect. We’ll give you the practice required for perfection and simulate the interview style of your target school.

  • Step 05
    Accepted

    Our job is done when you accept an offer of admission. We help with everything from getting off the wait-list to selecting among the schools you’ve been admitted to.

    Keep in touch to let us know when you get into your dream school!
